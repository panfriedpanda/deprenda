The Beginning

More Than
an Owner

He did not simply run Rimowa. He understood it — its DNA, its engineering rigour, its particular blend of German precision and cosmopolitan ambition. During his tenure, he shaped the brand's direction in ways that still resonate in every groove of an aluminium shell today.

But alongside that work, quietly and with great care, he built something else: a personal archive of the brand's most exceptional expressions. Cases that marked milestones. Colorways made in tiny numbers. Pieces from collaborations that never quite reached the public. Prototypes. Gifts. Curiosities.

Together they form one of the most complete and authentic records of Rimowa's evolution that exists anywhere in the world.

"These were never meant to be sold. They were meant to be kept. But now the time feels right to let them find new homes — with people who understand what they are."
— The de Prenda Family
The Journey

A Life with Rimowa

1980s
The Connection Begins

The de Prenda family's relationship with Rimowa takes root — a growing appreciation for the brand's craftsmanship and uncompromising design philosophy.

2000s
The Collection Deepens

As the brand grows internationally, so does the personal archive. Limited editions, airline collaborations, and anniversary pieces are carefully preserved.

2025
The Collection Opens

For the first time, a selection of these extraordinary pieces is offered to the world — from Château de Prenda, Belgium.

1990s
Ownership & Stewardship

The family becomes deeply involved in Rimowa at the ownership level — now with unique access to the rarest expressions the brand produces.

2010s
100+ Pieces Preserved

The collection reaches over one hundred individual pieces. Many have never been seen outside the walls of the château.

What We Believe

Three Principles

I
Objects Have Memory

A great suitcase is not just a container. It carries the decisions, the materials, the moments of its era. These pieces remember things that no catalogue records.

II
Provenance Is Everything

We cannot tell you these are rare and expect you to simply believe it. We can show you the documentation, open the doors of the château, and let the evidence speak for itself.

III
The Right Buyer

We are not in a rush. These pieces deserve owners who understand them. We would rather take more time and find the right person than sell quickly to the wrong one.
